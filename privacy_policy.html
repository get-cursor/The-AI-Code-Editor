Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide our intelligent creation services. This includes account details, usage patterns, and technical data that enables human-machine symbiosis in digital architecture.

Personal information is collected only when voluntarily provided through registration, contact forms, or service interactions. We process this data to deliver, maintain, and improve our platform capabilities.

Cookies Usage

Our platform uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, maintain session integrity, and analyze platform performance. Essential cookies enable core functionality, while analytics cookies help us understand usage patterns.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality and personalization features.

Analytics and Performance

We employ analytics tools to monitor platform performance, user engagement, and system optimization. This data helps us refine the digital architecture and improve intelligent creation workflows.

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized where possible. We use these insights to enhance the symbiotic relationship between human creativity and machine capabilities.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our infrastructure employs encryption, secure protocols, and regular security audits.

While we strive for maximum security, no digital system is completely impervious. We continuously update our security practices to address emerging threats and maintain data integrity.

Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. These services operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage reviewing their terms before interaction.

We carefully select partners who maintain high privacy standards and limit data sharing to what is necessary for service delivery.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection regulations, you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access your personal data and obtain copies of information we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances
  • Object to processing of your personal information for specific purposes
  • Request restriction of processing in certain situations
  • Data portability to transfer your information to another service
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities regarding data handling

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill service purposes, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Retention periods vary based on data type and regulatory requirements.

Upon account deletion or data removal requests, we securely erase information within reasonable timeframes, except where legal retention is mandated.
